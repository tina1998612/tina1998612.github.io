Bio

Lee Ting Ting is the co-founder and CTO of Turing Chain and an invited scholar at UC Berkeley Blockchain Lab. Previously, she worked at Microsoft as a Data and AI consultant. She is a technical writer on Medium with 3.4k subscribers worldwide. Ting Ting has published three blockchain papers in the authoritative forum ACM, IEEE, and international journal CPE. She was awarded the 2018 Best Paper at CryBlock in Munich, Germany.
